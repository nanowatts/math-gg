    MATH CLASS

    WHO WE ARE

    "Clans are people. Focus on that." - DeeJ

    Math Class is a gaming collective built on the passion, talent, and the tremendous character of its members. We're here to play games and attain the most badass achievements that the world of geekdom offers.

    SQUAD GOALS

    Games are about people. Our people have been where it counts and made it back victorious. Those homecomings yield many a trophy to hoist high.

    Math Class is deeply entrenched in gaming culture, and our accomplishments reflect it. We've been featured on Bungie, Kotaku, IGN and Polygon. Members of Math Class also retain Twitch and MLG partnerships.

    THE ROSTER

    We are fathers, mothers, students, teachers, doctors, pirates, engineers and champions. Our members span Canada, The Netherlands, Britain, Germany and the good ol' USA.
